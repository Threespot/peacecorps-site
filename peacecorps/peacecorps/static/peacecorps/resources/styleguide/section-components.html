2Components #permalink

Components are small modular piecies of functionality and styling that can be used to build up larger modules.

2.1Banner #permalink

A full width to wrap banner of differnt colors and sizes. This differs from full banner in that it doesn't span the whole width of the viewport. The banner has a top line of a different color.

Examples
Default styling

Some content in the banner

.banner--primary
Banner with primary color border and dark neutral background.

Some content in the banner

.banner--secondary
Neutral background with secondary border and white foreground. .banner--support -- Support background with .75 opacity, medium neutral border and white forground.

Some content in the banner

.banner--neutral_dark
Deprecated

Some content in the banner

.banner--dark_transparent
Secondary dark color with transparency on white.

Some content in the banner

.banner--no_line
A border without the usual top line.

Some content in the banner

.banner--short
A shorter version of the banner.

Some content in the banner

.banner--sitewide_notification
A specific banner used on the top of the site serving as a notification.

Some content in the banner

  <h4>Some content in the banner</h4>
</div>
